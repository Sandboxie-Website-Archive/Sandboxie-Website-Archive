Bitcoin: protect your wallet

If you use Bitcoins and the Bitcoin client:

By extending the [GlobalSettings] section of your sandboxie.ini with

Code: Select all

ClosedFilePath=%AppData%\Bitcoin\
you block read and write access to your complete Bitcoin wallet folder for all sandboxed programs.
