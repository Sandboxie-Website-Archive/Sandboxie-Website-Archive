Re: Windows 10 64 bit won't print in Sandboxie v 5.12

Are you getting any notifications from SBIE about giving access outside the sandbox to print?? You should have a pop up box.

Anytime you need to print, that has to go outside of the sandbox, you can try adding this to your SBIE .ini file:

Code: Select all

OpenFilePath=c:\windows\system32\spool\printers\*
