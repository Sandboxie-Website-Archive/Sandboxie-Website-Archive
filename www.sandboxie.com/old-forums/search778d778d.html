Re: How to stop immediate recovery of XMTM files only?

.
Here is my solution:

1. Sandbox > DefaultBox > Sandbox Settings > Recovery > Immediate Recovery
2. Check "Enable Immediate Recovery"
3. Click "Add Type"
4. Type ".xmtm"
5. Click OK
.
