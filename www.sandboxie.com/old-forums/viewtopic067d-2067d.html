Posted: Sun Apr 12, 2009 5:46 pm
by wraithdu
Yes, you don't understand what that tool is doing. It is meant to remove the 'BoxExpandedView' lines that were left over in the Sandboxie.ini file after completely removing a sandbox (not just emptying it). So if you use SB Control to remove sandbox Test3, then start SBDelete, you won't see Test3 listed in the view, which is correct. If you then run the tool the 'BoxExpandedView' line that corresponds to Test3 will be removed from your INI. It will not remove anything that is listed in the view on the main tab, regardless of the checkbox state.
