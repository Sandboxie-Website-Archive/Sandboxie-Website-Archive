We do not test Steam.
Games like that are to be installed ON YOUR host & then (possibly) run as sandboxed. This is the recommended Sandboxie way & has been for over a year.
We do not guarantee any program will install into the SB. While that is a feature, it was never a core intention and cannot apply to games that require unfeathered resources on the host.
There is no way around that.
The Trust no program doesn't mean Steam games.
If you're paying and using a legitimate service, there is nothing to not trust.

We are not currently looking at any fixes for Steam. The Steam client works, Steam detects SBIE as a cheat program. We haven't tested Steam or updated SBIE directly for Steam use in over a year. At the moment, that's simply not on our roadmap.
