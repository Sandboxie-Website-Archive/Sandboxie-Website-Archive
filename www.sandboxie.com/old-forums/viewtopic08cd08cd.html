Post by Craig@Invincea » Tue Nov 17, 2015 8:20 am

It's not SBIE or Invincea.

It's how KIS interacts with windows and how it mimics what SBIE does, thus it directly conflicts with SBIE. Our Devs have looked at the issue, and it's not feasible at this time.

We will not modify SBIE so that a A/V program will work. There are many other options out there.

Maybe in further releases of KIS, they will be less obtrusive?

However, A/V programs are suggested to be used in "addition to" SBIE, not as a primary defense when online. It should be SBIE as primary, then a A/V you choose. We use Windows Defender (Live Essentials). It works just fine with what it needs to do & identify threats in your sandbox (which you can just delete).
