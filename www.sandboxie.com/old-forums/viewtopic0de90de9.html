[.03] Fixes/improvements to DDE across the sandbox

  • Quote

Post by tzuk » Mon Jul 01, 2013 7:46 am

Version 4.03.03 brings back some important functionality that was missing in version 4.02 and that is DDE communication across the sandbox.

It should now be possible to click on documents that are associated with forced programs and the documents should open correctly.

It should also be possible to use Bookmark Buddy out of the sandbox with a Firefox in the sandbox.

Thank you for your patience with this.
