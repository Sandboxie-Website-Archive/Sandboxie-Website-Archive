Hello CBA,
1. In 58 at every SB launch I get a message "Firefox updates" - why?
This happens because you haven't updated Firefox outside Sandboxie. You need to update programs outside the Sandbox, then delete the contents of your Sandbox and re-launch them.
2. I can´t add bookmarks in SB - is that normal? On the other side I do have bookmarks and it´s folders in my sandboxed Firefox - the same that I had in my Firefox before SB. How do I add bookmarks?
You need to explicitly tell Sandboxie that you want to it to modify your host. Otherwise, the info stays in the Sandbox. Please see: https://www.sandboxie.com/FirefoxTips
