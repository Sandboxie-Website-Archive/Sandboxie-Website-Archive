I'm a long-time user of Sandboxie and purchased a Personal License back on 6/20/2008 and another Personal License on 10/13/2011. I bought the second one just because I like the program so much and wanted to support Ronen Tzur in his efforts to sustain it. I still have the receipts for the licenses.

Both licenses were marketed as lifetime that could be used on any machines I own.

1) Are the Personal Licenses that I purchased from Ronen Tzur being honored by the new owners of Sandboxie under the same marketing terms (i.e. lifetime on any mackines I own)?

2) The 2008 Personal License that I bought came with a "Registration Key" consisting of 7 digits (alpha and numeric). The 2011 Personal License that I bought came with a "Product Key" consisting of 25 digits (much like a Windows Product Key). Assuming the answer to my first question is that Invincea is honoring my Personal Licenses, does it matter which Registration Key or Product Key I use?

Re: New computer and Registration Key

Post by Craig@Invincea » Sun Jan 03, 2016 1:29 pm

The "new" longer reg keys were put into place by Ronen, not Invincea back in v3.7x.

As of SBIE v3.76 does require that new key. We are on stable version 5.06. If you have Windows 10, you must use version 5 or better.

In fact, because of Windows updates in Oct, anything below v5.05 will not work.

Please email support@sandboxie.com with order details and the original 7 digit key. Those keys are linked to the newer 25 digit keys per Ronen. We can then get your the newer key for future installs.
