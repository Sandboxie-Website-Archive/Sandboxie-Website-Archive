Post by GwGix » Sun Sep 18, 2016 2:02 pm

Further to my previous post: I can now confirm that in Windows 10 x64, with Sandboxie version 5.12… (and F-SecureSAFE)

With the current workaround – Sometimes sending and receiving will cause a freeze of Outlook – but nothing appears to get lost fortunately...

Another significant thing to remember – is to make sure that Outlook is opening to one of your Inboxes, and not opening to the "OutlookToday page".
I have found that if you have Outlook set to open to the "Outlook Today page" – Outlook will freeze on trying to open in a sandbox.

The answer is simple though: just take computer off-line, open Outlook outside of a sandbox, and set Outlook (Advanced options) to open to one of your inboxes on startup, instead of the default "Outlook Today page"...
After doing this, you will find that with the workaround given, it is now possible to use Outlook in a sandbox again!
