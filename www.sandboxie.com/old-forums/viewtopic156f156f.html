Post by Craig@Invincea » Wed Jun 29, 2016 9:40 pm

GData Internet Security, Malwarebytes Anti-Malware Home (Premium), Actual Window Menu
3rd Party security programs have a long long history of working, then not working, then working again. We always recommend those be disabled or removed. Their update could have broken something, or something we did to fix a program we support many have other ramifications. The only 3rd party program recommend is Windows Defender.

As for AWM, that program and programs like it have long history of problems dating back to before 2009. We spend a lot of time on that fix, and it may not be a permanent fix, We simply might say you can't use that with SBIE. The core of SBIE is to protect you online, AWM doesn't assist SBIE in that.

And what @Bo stated is very true. That could be the case, it may not be.

But again, if 5.12 wasn't working, I wouldn't be working, Curt, Bo and tens of thousands of other users would be in the same boat. A non working copy of SBIE.
