Post by tzuk » Thu Dec 27, 2012 5:19 am

Error 740 means "administrator privileges are required", and Chrome does ask for admin privileges when setting itself as a default browser.

Your Chrome in the sandbox may keep thinking that it has to set itself as a default browser, even though Chrome outside the sandbox has already taken care of that.

So my advice is to delete the contents of your sandbox to make sure the two versions of Chrome are in sync.

I think maybe you right-clicked Properties on Start.exe (or some shortcut that leads to Start.exe, like "Run any program under Sandboxie") and then you went to Compatibility tab and selected "Run this program as an administrator."

And now you get errors that Windows wants to elevate Start.exe to administrator privileges.
