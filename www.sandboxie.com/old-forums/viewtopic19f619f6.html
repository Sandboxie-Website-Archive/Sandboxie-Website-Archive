Post by bo.elam » Mon May 22, 2017 5:17 pm

Hi MicFem, I reread your posts and I see there are a bunch of things you haven't tried.

1. Try a new Firefox profile.
2. Disable Hardware acceleration (Options>Advanced>General) and Webgl (open about:config, toggle webgl.disabled to true) in Firefox.
3. Uninstall Firefox, do a clean install
4. Switch to another browser, Not Chrome or anything like Chrome but to a browser similar to Firefox (like Palemoon, Seamonkey).
5. For testing disable or better, uninstall Gdata. Years ago, the behavior module in Gdata conflicted with SBIE. It had a name, I dont remember what it was but disabling it was a good workaround.
6. Uninstall/reinstall Sandboxie. Dont keep settings.
