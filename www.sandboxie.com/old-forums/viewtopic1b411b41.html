@BatManCatMan
The Devs saw your post, and I can also confirm that we are not seeing freezing in Chrome. Are we seeing random crashes in Chrome in Win 10? Yes. In torture testing it's happening. But not all the time. As for the freezing, that can be several things. From A/V Installed (tool bar or add-ons) to a bum install of Chrome.

But freezing, No. And Chrome is my default browser in testing. I'm in it all the time...and with that, I have not seen the crashing that's been confirmed, but the tests and Devs are aware and it's being looked at, but I certainly have not seen freezing. Nor have we had any reports of that sort of issue.

If you want to provide me of software you have installed, you can do this:
Open Power Shell (as admin) and type:

Get-WmiObject -Class Win32_Product | Select-Object -Property Name > C:\Software\PCapps.txt

The PCapps.txt will be created listing your software, Please email that to support@sandboxie.com
