The Software vendor may prohibit the same instance of the game running at once. Sandboxie does not had you from the software detecting this. Sandboxie is well known, and many vendors do not permit it.
You may also be violating the software's EULA and/or the ToS.

You may try a VM. Sandboxie is not that either.
