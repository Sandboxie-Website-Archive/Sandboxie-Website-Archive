Hello,
The issue with me happens in all sandboxies. Me and my friend tried older versions of sandboxie too. Outside sandboxie it just won't happen.
It takes awhile after you start playing in a VAC secure server for the error to pop, but after 5-20 minutes it always happened. We logged in 20 accounts in a casual match, after 5 minutes a few accounts started getting the error and slowly all the others did too. It did not happen until sometime last month.
We're doing a few tests in other operating systems and other nvidia drivers, hoping to find a way to fix the problem, but so far we're unable to even end a competitive match before the error pops. We can only continue with the match outside sandboxie.

Note: Spectating a match won't cause the error, only when you're playing on a VAC secure server.

Also, to add to my last post, the issue "Your connection isn't reliable to matchmaking servers" is not related to this problem in any way. This problem exists since many months ago, and is easily fixed by running steam in administrator mode. However, I do not know of any fix around the VAC authentication error problem.
Me and my friend will connect 20 accounts to casual again, 18 sandboxed, and will see how long it takes for them to be disconnected.


Update:
Me and my friend tried connecting 20 accounts, 18 sandboxed, to a VAC secure server. All of them disconnected, except the 2 running normally. The whole process of 18 accounts being disconnected took around 20 minutes.
Later on, we tried 1 sandboxed account each of us on another VAC secure server. After 5 minutes my friend's account disconnected, after 5 more minutes mine did.
And again: Just watching a match won't cause this error, you must be playing on a VAC secure server. VAC system won't check if you're hacking if you're just watching.

My conclusion: The account disconnects whenever VAC tries to scan your game, for some reason sandboxed CSgo's won't respond to VAC system. Most likely the VAC system had an update that bugged sandboxie's communication with VAC servers.
And to finish, this is the link that the game sends you when the VAC authentication error hits https://support.steampowered.com/kb_art ... -ilzv-2837
Hopefully my posts helps to find what exactly is causing this. Me and my friend purchased sandboxie solely for CSgo. If any more information is required, I'll be glad to help.
