TDPsGM wrote:If I hide a "Message From Sandboxie" it says that it will not show me the message again in the future.

If I want to, how do I 'Unhide' messages that I have asked to be hidden in the past?
If you hide a message, that particular message will not be shown again unless you select "Forget hidden messages".

Sandboxie control>Configure>Forget hidden messages

You can read about SBIE messages, how to hide them and how to show them again in this links.

http://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?Conf ... forgetmsgs
