Post by SolarisGrim » Sun Oct 15, 2017 9:45 pm

I believe I was the first to report this months ago. I use Acrobat Pro DC and the only way around it at least for now is to change settings within FF itself.
If you on latest FF 56.0

about:config

Switch security.sandbox.content.level from 3 to 2
Close Firefox
Relaunch and re-try printing.

Sandboxie is amazon and I wouldn't go without it now, so hopefully dev is working on that and few other issues e.g. audio
