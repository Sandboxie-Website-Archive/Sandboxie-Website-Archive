Hello Creave,

The available options to force programs are:

https://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?ForceFolder
If you keep all your .mp4 files in the same location, then force folder will ensure that they always open Sandboxed.

https://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?ForceProcess
If you open your .mp4 files (for example) with VLC, then you can force VLC in a specific Sandbox , thus forcing them to always play Sandboxed.
