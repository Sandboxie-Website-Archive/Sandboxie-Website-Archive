We only directly support core browser functions (i.e, just the browser)
We only support a very, very limited amount extensions. Such as LastPass, there are just too many and it takes way too much resources to do.
We work are way down from the big 3 (IE, Chrome, Firefox) then to Opera and other forks of both Chrome and FF.

It's not something we would look at currently, but it will be noted.

Remember, this beta (and like any beta) only addresses problems listed in the change notes by the Dev. If it's not listed there, then we didn't address it. Unless you require a fix that was in one of the beta builds, you should stick with stable v5.10

What version of Windows are you using? 64 bit? What version (bit) of Opera are you using?
