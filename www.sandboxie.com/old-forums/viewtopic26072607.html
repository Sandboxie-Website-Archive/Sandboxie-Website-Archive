Generically speaking, no. While it is possible to isolate areas and only allow 'certain' things to see or interact with them - there is nothing atm, to my knowledge, which works exactly as you desire. I could easily use that scenario myself but sadly it is not how things are in the cards, atm. The workarounds I tend to use are GLOBAL [ClosedFilePath(s)] with an [!]exception for whatever program.exe or <ProcessGroup> actually needs access followed by any other relevant rules that may help lock that particular box down inside its own.
Sort of a deny everything except this and this extra rules for related boxes which might require many other rules of their own.
