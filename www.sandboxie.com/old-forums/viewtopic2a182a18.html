by Sam777
The same problem started happening with Firefox, too (firefox starting before sandboxie). After additional troubleshooting I figured out what was happening and would like to share what I did.

It actually doesn't have anything to do with Chrome or Firefox. Apparently, Comodo HIPS can cause Sandboxie itself to temporally delay on initializing a sandbox. HIPS is disabled by default when you install Comodo, so unless you enabled it this solution might not be for you.

To fix it, go into HIPS settings and remove all instances of Sandboxie and click OK. For me there were 3 references. Then open HIPS again and click ADD to open a new rule. Then click on BROWSE - RUNNING PROCESSES. Another window will open. Click on SbieCtrl.exe and click OK. Under Ruleset select allowed application and click OK. Now the sandbox should start without being delayed.
