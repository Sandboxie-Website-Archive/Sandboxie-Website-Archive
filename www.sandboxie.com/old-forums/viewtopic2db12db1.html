Post by btm » Wed Oct 21, 2015 5:54 pm

It's likely because CS:GO uses valve anti-cheat and relies on the steam service being actually installed on the system running and accessible. You'll have to have steam installed outside of sbie for it to even have a chance at working as sbie doesn't allow services or drivers installed in a box. I experienced this with Left 4 Dead 2 (It would get to the menu etc fine) and my sbie only based steam whenever I started joining a multiplayer game it would ctd, no errors just bam, gone. Short of actually installing steam outside of sbie I opted to just reboot and play it on my isolated gaming-only OS. I was never willing to install steam, origin, etc outside of sbie on my main OS so I can't help at all if you already have steam installed (maybe re-install it outside of sbie?) on the system and still have these errors. =( It's also possible there may be some 'resources access monitor' clues to help figure out what you need to allow if you have steam installed on the system with the service running outside sbie.
