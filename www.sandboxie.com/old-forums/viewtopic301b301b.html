*\BaseNamedObjects*\CS_Winner*
*\BaseNamedObjects*\CS_Winner_ContentDownload_License
*\BaseNamedObjects*\CS_WINNER_*
*\BaseNamedObjects*\_PH_PERFORMANCE

If those don't work, you can try adding more following the same pattern, just replace the portion after *\BaseNamedObjects*\
