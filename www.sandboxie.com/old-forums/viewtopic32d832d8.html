Hello Simplelife123abc,

You can create groups in order to address the 36 labels limitation (as stated above by Brummelchen):
https://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?SandboxMenu#layout

Open Sandboxie Control ---> Sandbox ---> Set Layouts and Groups.
By default, "Top" is the only group listed. Right click on it to create new groups.
Right click on your Sandboxes to move them to the desired group.
Each new group will be assigned a label, and then will be able to hold 36 labels inside of them.

For Feature requests, please visit this section:
