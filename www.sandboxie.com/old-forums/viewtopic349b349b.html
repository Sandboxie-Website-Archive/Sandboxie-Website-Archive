Well personally, I think unsandboxing the sandbox would be stupid, but, one toggle key I would like to see, would be for "Terminate all programs"..

The way it works, you need to use the system tray icon to access "Terminate all programs", well that really sucks when you encounter a huge barage of popups or something that keep you from being able to access the tray icon!

But instead, if we had a hotkey, then no matter how much junk we are being flooded with, we could just click "Ctrl-Shift-t" (just an example) and it would terminate all programs without using the tray icon!
