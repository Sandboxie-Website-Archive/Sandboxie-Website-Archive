Master_Blaster, you refer to different questions:
Master_Blaster wrote: but i need to know it is really lifetime and not 1 year
The answer was given by chrisc@invincea in the posting linked by Bo.
The answer is yes - it is really lifetime. You can also see it in the order form whose link you posted yourself: Under the product name ("Sandboxie - Personal License") it says: "Lifetime License".

Your second + third question is:
Master_Blaster wrote: i can use it on more than one pc at the same time ? (for my need)
price is 34,27 euros totally end price no matter what ?
The answer to both questions is yes (if with "no matter what" you mean the use on more than one of your own PCs). That you can use it on any PC you own is not only (indirectly) part of the statement of chrisc@invincea. It is the consequence of the End User License Agreement (EULA) (quoted also by chrisc@invincea in his above posting). The EULA says:
The Author grants You a personal, limited, non-exclusive, non- transferable license to access, install, download, copy the
Software, or otherwise benefit from using the functionality of the Software, on any number of computers you own, provided that You adhere to all of the terms and conditions of this Agreement.
(full text here: http://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?EndU ... eAgreement)

The important part are the words "on any number of computers you own".

So you also need not worry about this:
Master_Blaster wrote: yes but someone told that they will be solving it, those mass illegal licensing.
Yes, that's also what chrisc@invincea mentioned. But this refers to the future. Maybe in the future they will change their terms (for future customers). But if you buy your license now, the relevant terms are the one which apply to your purchase.

Your next question is again something different:
Master_Blaster wrote: i would buy that invicea, BUT it doesnt support multiple sandboxes at same time. i dont know why !
The support of multiple sandboxes (using them at the same time) is a feature exclusively of the paid version (1 year license or lifetime license [plus the 10 winners of a lifetime license]). So of course it will not work for you if you use only the free version. You will benefit from this feature only after buying a license.

And finally:
Master_Blaster wrote: also i won't be able to buy it till 16th may.
is there any other coupon or promotion for same thing ? which has longer time to apply?
This is something that only the staff of Invincea can tell you. But as originally you were interested in buying until May 16th: 3 days remain for you to buy.

Post by chrisc@invincea » Tue May 13, 2014 11:51 am

We have two markets that we're supporting at Invincea.

Sandboxie is being supported and enhanced to serve the consumer market. Sandboxie has capabilities that are not available in the Invincea FreeSpace product that were developed specifically by the original Sandboxie author (Ronen) to support the use cases of consumers. (I use Sandboxie at home on multiple computers!)

Invincea, the company, and its products are developed for the business market specifically for malware prevention of advanced threats that uniquely target business customers. This market has unique requirements and capabilities that are available in FreeSpace and other Invincea products which are not available in Sandboxie.

Think of this like Honda. Honda creates small cars and motorcycles for consumers, and creates vans and trucks (and other industrial equipment) for businesses. Honda makes vehicles in both markets that get you from point A to point B, but each vehicle is specifically built for the needs of the audience.

(If anyone has a better example, please post!)
