Rodalsa, the Sandbox folder in C: drive is where Sandboxie keeps track of changes. Sandboxies role is to keep whats inside from getting out. But you can go inside the folder. Programs outside the sandbox, like antiviruses, can detect viruses as they arrive in the sandbox folder. Read this FAQ. I know you are not asking about viruses but this FAQ mentions the sandbox folder.
http://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?FAQ_Virus
