Hello sakloss,

What is the application that you are using, and what's the version of it?
Is there any antivirus software installed?
What are the steps to reproduce the problem? Are you receiving an error message? What's happening, exactly?

As explained before, we do not support Vista.
Regarding Sandboxie v4.04, that version is deprecated. Please download the latest version of Sandboxie:
https://www.sandboxie.com/DownloadSandboxie

And then create a new Sandbox to re-test the issue:
https://www.sandboxie.com/SandboxMenu#create

If you are very new to Sandboxie, I strongly recommend that you follow this tutorial to familiarize with its features:
https://www.sandboxie.com/GettingStarted

To post a copy of your Sandboxie.ini, open Sandboxie control and to Configure--> Edit configuration
Copy-paste the contents in the forum.
Highlight the copied text and click on the "</>" button to format it (so that it is easy to read).
