Thanks for the reply!
That utility is pretty awesome, and will be a nice tool to throw into my toolbox. However, it doesn't quite solve my problem
That DLL blocks programs in the sandbox from accessing outside processes, but unfortunately sandboxie runs a few programs inside of the sandbox, such as SandboxieDcomLaunch.exe and SandboxieRpcSs.exe. Harmony actually doesn't scan any processes outside of the sandbox that I know of right now.

As far as my intentions, I will say that you are partly correct- I do intend to bypass the security software. Unfortunately, Harmony comes with a lot of overhead and severely hampers skill spamming by a human, and as well I would like to enable basic macros. I can use comodo firewall's sandbox for this, but I rather like the idea of Sandboxie because it's a much more simple and less bloated program.
I also don't like the idea of this software going through my files and processes. This is a small server and I'd rather be safe than sorry when it comes to personal data and such.
