Hi xRemix, you should be able to run most programs that you ran sandboxed in W7 in W10. I am using same programs in both systems and running them sandboxed without issue.

Important: What are the programs you are having issue running andboxed in W10?

Be aware, Universal apps and Edge are not compatible with Sandboxie. So, you cant run this type of programs sandboxed.

I updated a little while ago my W10 to 1709 16299.125, and all related to SBIE appears to be well.

I got my W10 five months ago, I expected problems but to my joy, I have found none related to Sandboxie. You should be OK. Using WD instead of another SV is a good idea in W10.
