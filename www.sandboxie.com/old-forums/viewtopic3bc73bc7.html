Hello itayo,

I downloaded 1Password, the latest version is 6.7.457, created and account and logged in.
I added the Chrome plugin and was able to repro the problem usign the latest SBIE beta 5.21.7 - Release Candidate

Heres'a workaround:
Right- click on your Sandbox --> Sandbox Settings--> Resource Access--> File Access --> Full Access
Hit "Add" and paste the following:
*\device\namedpipe\1password*
Apply and Okay your way out.
Configure --> Reload Configuration
Delete the contents of your Sandbox

After that, launch 1password on your host and then launch Chrome inside your modified Sandbox.
I tested it with existing accounts, as well as creating a new entry from a Sandboxed browser and did not find any issues.

Regards,
Barb.-
