Post by Barb@Invincea » Mon Mar 19, 2018 1:36 pm

Hello opera,

Does the problem occur in a new sandbox with default settings?
Sandbox --> Create new Sandbox
Do not copy settings from existing Sandboxes

Does the issue occur without the Antivirus software installed?

Is the QWAVE template enabled?
Right click on your Sandbox
Sandboxe Settings --> Applications--> Miscellaneous
Ensure Allow direct access to qWave driver (Google Hangouts) is selected

Any chance Screen Readers template is on?:
Right-click on your Sandbox--> Sandbox Settings --> Applications---> Accessibility
Un-check Screen Readers: JAWS, NVDA...
Hit Apply, hit Ok.

Otherwise, please post your configuration file:
Configure -->Edit configuration
Copy/paste the output
Highlight the output and click on the "</>" button to format it in the forums.

I recall a similar issue reported in the past, and this workaround did the trick for some users (only do this if none of the above help)
viewtopic.php?p=124860#p124860

Regards,
