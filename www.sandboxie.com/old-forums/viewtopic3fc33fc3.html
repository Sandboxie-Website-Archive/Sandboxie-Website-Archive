Post by wraithdu » Mon Apr 27, 2009 10:10 am

I'm guessing you already have a local version of Sandboxie installed and running. You cannot run the portable version with a locally installed version of SB.

Or you renamed the installer... don't do that. It checks for a running "SandboxiePortable.exe". So if you renamed the installer, it will detect itself and abort.

Post by wraithdu » Tue May 12, 2009 10:11 am

I suppose I misspoke there, or changed something after that. Anyway, from the current readme -
Except for the %SANDBOX% token which is automatically added to the
end of the 'FileRootPath' setting, there can be only one other token
in the path, it must exist at the beginning of the path, and it
cannot be a Sandboxie specific token such as %SID%.
So, SB specific tokens (%SID%, %USER%, %SESSION%) are not supported, and you can have only one other token at the beginning of the path. I may revise that last restriction at some point in the future.

Your path didn't work for one and both of those reasons.
