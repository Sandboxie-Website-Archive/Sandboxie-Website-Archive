Hello czajaPL,

I can repro using the Screen readers tempalate, which causes all sort of problems.
Please disable it by going to Configure --> Software Compatibility. Un-check Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA...). Hit Ok.

Or, if you have manually added it to your Sandbox (not globally):
Right-click on your Sandbox--> Sandbox Settings --> Applications---> Accessibility
Un-check Screen Readers: JAWS, NVDA...
Hit Apply, hit Ok.

Re-try accessing the site after clearing the contents of your Sandbox.

Regards,
Barb.-
