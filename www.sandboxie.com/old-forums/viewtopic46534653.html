I have since managed a workaround where however i have to not have in this example a browser open within Sandboxie but open outside it and therefore the Macro-ToolWorks Pro will work in terms of me making the macro program and thus now i have the program functioning i then open the browser in sandboxie and then when running the macro it works whilst browser in sandboie.

The annoyance is that when having to tweek the macro program which is quite a job i have to always have the browser out of sandboxie and then when i feel i have made progress to the macro i then have to try the macro out whilst the browser is in sandboie.

Anything running in Sandboxie will not allow Macro-ToolWorks Pro to work alongside with it unless as i explained above. Meaning when you want to use keyboard keys such as alt ctrl etc. the Macro-ToolWorks Pro did not write it whilst recording the macro.
