Barb,

Thanks for pointing me in the right direction.

The culprit was Norton Security. Even though the Sandboxie installer scanned clean and was clean in Norton's Insight, Norton blocked it and did not alert, even though Norton is set up to alert. Furthermore, there was no Norton record of blocking the Sandboxie installation. I simply went straight to whitelisting the Sandboxie installer in Norton , and then Sandboxie installed. Have not seen anything like this before from Norton or any other AV, but I'm glad this is now solved. Thanks again.
