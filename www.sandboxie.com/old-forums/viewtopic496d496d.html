using Sandboxie in a Ramdisk

Posted: Wed Nov 14, 2007 10:57 pm

First of all a big thank you to Tzuk for a great software, it's been ages since I last used Adaware/SpyBot Search & destroy and various cleaning softwares to get rid of useless internet junk and privacy issues.

I have all my sanboxes and portable browser (Avant) in a RamDisk which gets wiped clean after each boot, anything I download to keep I recover straight to my hard drive.
Definite browsing speed improvements when using multiple tabs (wireless broadband)! and no need to clear the sandbox as it's all clean and new after each boot.
