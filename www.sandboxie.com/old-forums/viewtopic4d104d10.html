hi

my c: drive is nearly no space.

how do i move sandboxie install folder to other drive and still make it working (including all sandboxie settings reserved)???

thanks.

Posted: Thu Nov 29, 2012 6:02 am
by tzuk
This is easiest if you don't need the contents in your sandboxes.

Delete the contents of all your sandboxes (Sandbox menu > Delete Contents).
Then go to Sandboxie Control > Sandbox menu > Set Container Folder.
Select folder on another disk, and you're done.
