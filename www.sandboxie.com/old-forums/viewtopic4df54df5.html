Tetranitro, I can answer some of your questions. The RC recognizes your license. And you can run the installer over the top. If you choose "Overwrite", your settings are maintained. So you know, I use many sandboxes in my XP and W7, all programs and files that I run sandboxed are running great under 4.10 RC.

Since I never keep anything installed in a sandbox for more than a few hours and have never upgraded SBIE while having a program installed in a sandbox, I can not tell you if "data within the sandboxes" is saved when you upgrade your Sandboxie, perhaps someone who keeps programs installed in sandbooxes can tell us how that goes.
