Post by Syrinx » Mon Oct 31, 2016 12:52 pm

In Sandboxie Control goto the Menu > File > Resource Access Monitor
This will open a box that should be blank if nothing is running inside sandboxie.
Run hexchat sandboxed and try to connect normally.

After you experience your issue go back to that box and select 'Copy contents to Clipboard and Close Window'
Then paste the contents here please.

Including more information concerning your other security/system software may also be helpful.

As for the addons, I was referring to hexchat as it has an area for them.
