UPDATE:

I can repro this issue on Win 7 x64 without SP1 or any updates.
The issue does not seem to affect up-to-date installations.

This problem seems to be related to the fact that outdated versions of Windows 7 do not have the ability to use SHA 256:
https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/securi ... 15/3033929

We are still investigating and looking at options to address this. In the meantime, the workarounds would be to update your Windows installation as explained on the link above, or use the beta we posted on a previous response to this thread.

One more question:
Are you all using Clean installs of Win 7, or you have upgraded from Win XP to 7?

Regards,
Barb.-
