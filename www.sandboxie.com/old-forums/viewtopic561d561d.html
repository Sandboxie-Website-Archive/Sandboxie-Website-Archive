Post by Curt@invincea » Tue Feb 25, 2014 6:11 pm

Bellzemos wrote:Good job! I just updated from Sandboxie v4.08 to v4.09 (beta) on both machines of mine (32-bit WinXP SP3 desktop & 64-bit Win7 SP1 laptop) and Firefox 27.0.1 works normal again (I also have the latest version of Avast running on both machines). How exactly did you guys solve the problem? There was, of course, no compensation in terms of security, right? Thank you!
Glad you like it. To solve the Avast incompatibility, we blocked Avast from injecting their Dll into sandboxed apps. Specifically, in templates.ini, we added ClosedFilePath for snxhk.dll and snxhk64.dll. I'm not sure what Avast is doing with these dlls, but it does not seem to interfere with their AV functionality.
