Re: Attribute error SBIE messages to a specific sandbox

Posted: Thu Jan 26, 2017 3:54 pm
by Curt@invincea
This is not as easy as it sounds.

1) Many error msgs are not associated with sandboxes.
2) Error msgs can originate in the Sbie driver, services, SbieDll, or SbieCtrl. When the error occurs, the sender of the error may not know which sandbox is involved.

If you have specific error msgs in mind, I will look into them. But SBIE1308 already contains the sandbox name, as do several other error msgs.
