wraithdu
Posts: 1410
Joined: Fri Jun 29, 2007 2:54 pm

  • Quote

Post by wraithdu » Tue Aug 25, 2009 7:38 pm

The launcher hinges on SbieCtrl.exe. It launches this process then waits for it to exit, which will trigger the 'Cleaning Up' (uninstallation). This means that for whatever reason SbieCtrl.exe is not running or is crashing on your machine, immediately triggering the clean up. Try a real installation of Sandboxie and see if it works on this machine.
