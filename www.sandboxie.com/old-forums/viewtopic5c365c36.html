djvam,

If your video cards are separate (not optimus or similar), you can try disabling your on-board one via Computer Management and see if that helps.
This is not something you will be able to do via Nvidia Control Panel.

If you have a different setup, you should see the something like "Manage 3D Settings" (inside Nvidia Control Panel), where you can select your preferred processor under Global settings.

See if any of those options work for you. Are your games defaulting to your Nvidia card outside Sbie?

Sandboxie is not a VM. Sandboxed applications use the same video card (and video settings) as host applications. Your video hardware is not aware that some applications are sandboxed and others are not.
