BSOD 0x00000018 - Reference by Pointer when 'Run Sandboxed' - Solution

  • Quote

Post by Trm2 » Sat Aug 19, 2017 11:37 am

For anyone experiencing this problem I have the solution IF you are running Malwarebytes Anti-Malware

Turn off Malwarebytes Anti-Malware Ransomeware Protection (right click on Icon in task tray and turn it off from there).

I hope this helps.
