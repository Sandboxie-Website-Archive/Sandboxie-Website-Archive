By default, programs don't have access to your host registry. Hives/hierarchy are created in the sandbox.
http://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?SandboxHierarchy

Access the the actual registry is only enabled if you allow that to happen. Once that happens, SBIE has no control or ability to monitor it.

You'd need a 3rd party program.
