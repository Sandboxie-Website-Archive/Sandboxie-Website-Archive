Hi Dan, you can tell if Firefox Multiprocess is on or not in Firefox Help/Troubleshooting information, there, look for Multiprocess Windows, it ll say Enabled or Disabled. You can also open Troubleshooting information by copying and pasting in Firefox Location bar (about:support), Click to Go.

And yes, no need to wonder nothing about updating Firefox or not. Firefox 54.0.1 is running great under Sandboxie.
Dan_Br0673 wrote: ↑
Sat Jul 22, 2017 4:16 pm
 Is that enough to ensure it is off, or do I also need security.sandbox.content.level=0
Theres no need to play with that setting above. You can enable or disable Multiprocess, do as you wish. Firefox runs great under SBIE with Multiprocess enabled or disabled.

Bo
