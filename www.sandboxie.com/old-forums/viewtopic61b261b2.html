The files need to be at least 1kb for Sandboxie to recover them. If you have 0kb files inside the Sandbox, you can manually recover them by copy-pasting them to the desired location as needed.

Regarding the upload comment, I am not sure what are you doing exactly, but you can manually copy 0kb files into Sandboxie using Windows Explorer Sandboxed.
