Just purchased a lifetime license

Posted: Sun Mar 17, 2013 9:27 pm
by SkedAddled
After having used Sandboxie for around 3 years, with WinXP Corporate 32-bit
and now with Win7 Ultimate 64-bit, I decided it was time for me to finally
contribute some supporting funds to the project, to help ensure continued
development efforts.

I will admit to having installed a 'cracked' version under XP x86, and it worked
extremely well for my purposes. I was also very poor at the time, although
this is hardly an acceptable excuse.

I am very glad to now be in a better financial position, being able to support
such projects as they benefit my own needs and uses. Sandboxie is an invaluable
tool for people like me, who frequently download such programs as casual games
which are often loaded with unwanted garbage like browser toolbars and the like.
SBIE makes it very trouble-free for me to try out and enjoy such programs
without them adding a bunch of additional garbage to my system, and equally
trouble-free for me to delete them when I'm done with them.
