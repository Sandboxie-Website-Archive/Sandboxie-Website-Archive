Post by Lode » Thu Mar 02, 2017 8:44 pm

Thank you for your response.

I'm using Sandboxie 5.16.

I had removed everything having to do with Opera in the Sandboxie settings, and then added it all again. (The same settings I have for IE11 and Chrome.) But still SBIE2203 would appear.
I also revisited the other thread. It tuns out that this is the situation with Alert3 now:

With IE11 I can have full Alert3 protection enabled -Safe browsing and Exploit mitigation- and the panel with this following line 6x repeated does not appear when surfing sandboxed: "SBIE2203 Failed to communicate with Sandboxie Service: *GUIPROXY_00000001 - opera.exe [FF000000]"
So Sandboxie, IE11, and Alert3 are completely compatible.

With Chrome I can leave Safe browsing enabled in Alert3, but not Exploit mitigation. Otherwise SBIE2203 appears.
With Epic SBIE2203 appears unless I have Safe browsing -except for Keystroke Encryption- and Exploit mitigation disabled in Alert3.
Same with Opera: only Keystroke Encryption enabled.

(I removed Firefox already some time ago and replaced it with Chrome.)

I can have all Alert3 options enabled for Chrome, Opera and Epic, but only by disabling sandboxing. I prefer to keep sandboxing enabled for them.
So I'm fine with this situation as it is now: full Sandboxie protection for all my browsers, and some options disabled in Alert3 for Chrome, Opera, and Epic. And thus no more SBIE2203 messages.
