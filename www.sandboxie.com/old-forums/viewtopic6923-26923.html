Post by AxemBlack » Wed Apr 15, 2015 1:40 am

I have been having this issue for a while, and I have checked these boards many time for solutions. The restarting the computer trick usually worked, but this time it was not doing the trick.
Curt@invincea wrote:As you found out, you can't run Sbie in safe mode. Just delete the sandbox folder from explorer. If it won't let you, then something has messed up the permissions on the folder.
This is what lead me to the solution. I registered an account on this forum just so I could possibly help someone who is having the same problem as I was. After trying to delete the sandboxie folder, it would not let me saying that it was probably open in another window (which I knew it wasn't). So Curt@invincea was right, I checked my folder permissions. You find your sandboxie folder, right click for properties, go to the security tab, and go through your groups and user names there. If you want to be thorough, for each one make sure that everything has a checkmark next to Allow (full control was not checked for me). Once I did that and hit apply, I didn't need to delete the folder. I just opened up sandboxie and tried to delete contents again and it worked no problem. Hope this helps anyone who was struggling as much as I was
