It seems that avast is starting a helper process to do the certificate check, and the process is running outside the sandbox in the ANONYMOUS LOGON account because that is the account that programs in the sandbox use in version 4. Unfortunately avast does not detect the failue in the helper process and Thunderbird remains stuck.

Version 4.05.01 unfortunately does not fix the problem, but points you to the Known Conflicts page.
