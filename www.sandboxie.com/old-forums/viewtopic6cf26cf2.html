I'm not sure of others' experiences with SBIE vs. GreenBorder, but I just wanted say I had been evaluating GreenBorder for a while, and the other day uninstalled it and reinstalled the latest version of SandboxIE.

Hands-down SB beats GB in terms of functionality, ease of use etc. No comparason. Love the app.

Just one example. GB rarely was able to install an app sandboxed fully - this may be due to overexcessive sb-ing, but SBIE allows me to fully install while keeping it all sandboxed - that functionality right there is critical to me.
