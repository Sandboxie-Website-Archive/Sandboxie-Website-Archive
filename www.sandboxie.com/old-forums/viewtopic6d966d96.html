nVidia drivers update v372.54 [DirectX]

  • Quote

Post by Craig@Invincea » Mon Aug 22, 2016 9:29 am

Currently, the newest nVidia drivers may not allow programs to run inside of the sandbox.

The work around is to disable by turning this off for now (GPU acceleration)
http://nvidia.custhelp.com/app/answers/ ... celeration [Then reboot]

The other option is to roll back the driver.

At this time, there is no ETA on a fix on the SBIE side. nVidia may update the driver product to that may fix the issue.

IF TURNING OFF THE PHYSX DOES NOT WORK, Please let us know what GPU card you are using.
