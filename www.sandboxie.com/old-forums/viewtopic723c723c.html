Hello nooby1 ,

The message that you are receiving is most likely the UAC prompt, which is normal and does not mean that Sandboxie is making changes to your host directly.
Here's a related thread:
http://forums.sandboxie.com/phpBB3/view ... ow#p116679

Sandboxed applications cannot make changes to your host machine (unless you specifically add it in the configuration).
Here's more information regarding Direct Access:
https://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?Res ... tings#file
