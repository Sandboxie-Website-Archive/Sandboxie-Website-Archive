Post by Craig@Invincea » Wed Oct 07, 2015 8:45 am

We have not noticed any of this in our testing. If we can't reproduce what you may have encountered, then we simply cannot. If you have more info (error messages, crash info, etc..etc) that helps.

We test core functionality. We can't possible test every-single browser addon or extension. We can say, the browser works.

SBIE v5.04 is no longer in Beta. It's the stable release that replaced v4.20.

Like any major upgrades (especially when you're dealing with a major OS upgrade) you should be mindful of any issues that may arise. We have SEVERAL HUNDRED THOUSAND of SBIE downloads in the past 9 months, and a few reported issues. Metric wise, given the amount of users that have downloaded and installed SBIE vs what we see here in the forum, we are way ahead of the game.

We try address general problems. We won't address individucal issues. If we see a reported pattern, then we have to look at it. Plus, per the ToS, Technical Support is not provided on SBIE (paid or free version http://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?RegisterSandboxie 7th bullet down).

We cannot guarantee everyone will have the same experience. That's impossible. I won't get into the hardware we use for testing, it varies and it's extensive. I can tell you we test with standard hardware the average user may have...your mileage may (and will) vary.

I can say that FF v.40.0.1 (including the beta channel) Chrome, Opera, Cyberfox, IE all function. Again, your mileage may vary.

If you want product updates (like a lot of products) it's best to check the source for any and all information about any product you may use.
