I am running win 7 64 bit and two days ago installed ie 11. Sandboxie 4.06 functions perfectly with it but ie 11 itself is buggy. I got black screens from failure of AMD graphics driver (driver stopped responding), although it is current. Many web sites that work with ie 10 give errors with ie 11 or don't work. I uninstalled ie 11 from the control panel windows update screen, but the uninstall itself was buggy. I unchecked the autoinstall box in the about IE screen and hope ie 11 doesn't get installed again.
