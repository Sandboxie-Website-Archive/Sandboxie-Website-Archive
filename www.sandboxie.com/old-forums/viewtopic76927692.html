Sandboxie already handles Evercookies. When you delete your sandbox, the cookies are gone. Canvas fingerprinting does not store anything on your computer. It uses your GPU to generate a "fingerprint" that may or may not be able to uniquely identify you. There are several browser add-ons that will block this.
