Hello 000,

Can you print from any other Sandboxed programs? (Notepad, Office apps, other browsers...)
Does the issue occur in a new Sandbox with default settings?
Is there any antivirus software involved?

Please provide the results of running Resource Access Monitor in a new Sandbox with default settings , while trying to print:
https://www.sandboxie.com/ResourceAccessMonitor
After copy/pasting the output in your response, highlight it and click on the "</>" button to format it.

Also, give this setting a try:
Right click on your Sandbox---> Sandbox settings ---> Restrictions ---> Network Files
Un-check "block network files and folders unless specifically open"
Hit Apply, OK
Configure --> Reload Configuration
Test printing again.

Barb.-
