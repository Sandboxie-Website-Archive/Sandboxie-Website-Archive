"Monitor" Magazine Picks Sandboxie

Posted: Fri Mar 14, 2008 11:25 am
by tzuk
Slovenian computer magazine Monitor awarded Sandboxie with the "Golden Monitor" award in a review that compared the following sandboxing products.

Altiris SVS
(censored) Pro
GeSWall Pro 2.7.1
Sandboxie 3.22
ShadowUser Pro 2.5
Virtual Sandbox 2

... "When we rebooted the computer (with several infected files still on the disk in the sandbox), none of
malware processes started. Neither sandboxed nor outside the sandbox. That means Sandboxie
successfully shielded Windows Registry from permanent entries of sandboxed malware. This is
impenetrability that hardly any other sandbox on our test can boast."
