Hi Jhen:
1. If your license has not been compromised, it should work with any Sandboxie version, older or newer than 5.18.
2. Click Help>Register Sandboxie >OK, Click Clear License Information.
3. Legacy licenses can be used on any computer you personally own. Otherwise, you can use it in one computer only. If it was purchased recently, then its the latter type. If it was purchased like 5 years ago, its legacy. To confirm what you got, you can send an email with your questions to:

support@sandboxie.com

4. Your license is not going to expire. Sandboxie checks the license against their server after 180 days or before, if and when you upgrade your Sandboxie version.

Bo
