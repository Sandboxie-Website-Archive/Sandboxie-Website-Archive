You don't need to recheck the Shell integration. By default, the Service will run.

Just because you don't have a tray icon does not mean that the SBIE services is not running. The tray icon is just a GUI. Do you get Any SBIE windows at startup?

1. Open Services (in Windows, type Services in the Windows search box) and check and see if Sandboxie service (SBIESVC) is running and it's set to automatic.
2. What happens when your right click on Firefox or Chrome and select "Run sandboxed"?
3. What happens when you click on "run browser sandboxed?"
