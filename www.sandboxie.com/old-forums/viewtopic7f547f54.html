Hello AV-3900 ,

It all depends on how do you set up your Sandbox and your browser on the host. If your browser is set to auto-delete cache when you close it, then the Sandboxed one will do the same thing. Also, browsers like Chrome allow you to log in to your google account, which is probably why you are still seeing your history even after deleting the contents of your Sandbox.
See this thread:
