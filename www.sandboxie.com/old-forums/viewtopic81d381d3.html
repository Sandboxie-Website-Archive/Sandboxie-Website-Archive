Are the files deleted absolutely securely deleted?
The short answer is YES.

You are asking if a deleted spyware program can recover its own deleted program. The answer is no because, by the time you clean sandbox, the spyware program has been terminated and deleted. It has ceased to exist in your computer. It is simply not there anymore, so it cannot ask the computer to recover its own files.
