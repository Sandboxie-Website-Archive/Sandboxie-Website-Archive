I did have a similar situation but it was months ago and it was Excel, it was similar in that windows installer opened once sandboxed and then a second time unsandboxed.

What it turned out to be was that before the second installer could do anything, I had to delete the files that were in Windows/Installer that had to do with office. An empty Windows/Installer folder and an UNsandboxed installer was what did it for me.

Post by cross » Sat Aug 25, 2007 6:49 am

I have the solution now:

I have reinstalled sandboxie but this time, I gave full administrator rights to the account I want to use outlook with and installed sandboxie under this account. Then I started outlook sandboxed and the error message didn't appear.

The problem was the following: Last time I installed sandboxie, I did this under an account with administrator rights but then I used outlook sandboxed with another account that had only user rights. For this reason, the error message appeared.
