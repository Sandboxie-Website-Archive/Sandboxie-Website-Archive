Post by Curt@invincea » Fri Mar 28, 2014 5:03 pm

Buster wrote:What are the reasons which caused the C0000142 errors?
They are all related to Dll injection (which is what broke your app, Buster). The Sandboxie 4.X Dll injection code is quite different than 3.X and is very complex. Tzuk primarily made these changes for Win 8 compatibility. He did an amazing job on this given that the NT loader is poorly documented by Microsoft.

Since these seemed to be the biggest problems for the largest number of people, for both Sandboxie and Freespace, we have been focusing almost exclusively on this area for weeks now.
