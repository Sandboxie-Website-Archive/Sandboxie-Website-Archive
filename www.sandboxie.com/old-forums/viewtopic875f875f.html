Hello dux,

1) You can choose to disable Sandboxie from the startup. Please see this entry:
https://www.sandboxie.com/ConfigureMenu#shell

2) Sandboxie installs its own drivers, we have a known conflicts section for your review:
https://www.sandboxie.com/KnownConflicts
I strongly recommend that you review our FAQ section to better understand what is Sandboxie and how does it interact with your system :
https://www.sandboxie.com/HelpTopics

3) That's the purpose of Sandboxie, to isolate files and programs so that they cannot modify your host. Here's a related thread with further info:
viewtopic.php?p=132354#p132354

4) You can block Internet access by following these steps:
https://www.sandboxie.com/RestrictionsSettings#internet
