When you get a Sandboxie message that says, "Could not move the sandbox folder out of the way. The object (file or folder) may be in use by another program....", usually is your antivirus locking files inside the sandbox while doing its thing as you close the sandboxd program and delete the sandbox.

Since you the know the problem goes away if you shut down Webroot, perhaps for now you can workaround the issue by excluding the Sandbox folder in C in Webroot Exclusions. You can also try adding to exclusions all of Sandboxies process that you find in the Sandboxie folder in Program files. Another thing you can do is at least temporarily uninstall Webroot and use Windows defender. I dont use an AV but I tested WD for about 36 hours after updating to FCU. I found no issue whatsoever with Sandboxie. I don't use Opera but Firefox is my browser. WD, Sandboxie and Firefox get along very well right now, as usual.
