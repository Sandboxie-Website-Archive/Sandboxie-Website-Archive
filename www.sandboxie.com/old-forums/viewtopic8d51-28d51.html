Post by Buster » Tue Jan 03, 2012 3:50 am

guest14789 wrote:::TO THE USER: You need to CUSTOMIZE THE NEXT PATH (inside and between quotes: " ") for your PC, e.g.: copy "C:\Sandbox\<YourUserName>\DefaultBox\RegHive" hive_1.bak /v /y > NUL

Sandbox NOTEPAD.EXE and then right-click Sandboxie Control. Click "DefaultBox > Explore Contents".

A Windows Explorer window will be opened in the defaultbox sandbox folder.

Copy and paste that path and replace it for "C:\Sandbox\<YourUserName>\DefaultBox".

That´s all.
