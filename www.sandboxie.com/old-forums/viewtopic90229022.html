If you want to provide me of software you have installed, you can do this:
Open Power Shell (as admin) and type:

Get-WmiObject -Class Win32_Product | Select-Object -Property Name > C:\Software\PCapps.txt

The PCapps.txt will be created listing your software, Please email that to support@sandboxie.com
