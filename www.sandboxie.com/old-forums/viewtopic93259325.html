Re: Settings?

by Guest10
Open the Sandboxie Control window. You can double-click the tray icon, or right-click and "Show Window".

Then either right-click on a sandbox, or click on the "Sandbox" menu item and hover the mouse cursor over the sandbox name, to see "Sandbox Settings".

Years ago, some of us suggested to Sandboxie's developer that "Sandbox Settings" be added to:
Right-click the tray icon > (hover mouse cursor over sandbox name) > add "Sandbox Settings"
He declined to do it, saying that he didn't want to add complexity to that menu.

When I was still new to Sandboxie, that's the first place that I would look for that item.
