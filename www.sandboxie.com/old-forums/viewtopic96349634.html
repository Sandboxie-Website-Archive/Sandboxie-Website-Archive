Hello joseph66,

It looks like you still have the RoboForm template activated
"Template=RoboForm"

You need to deactivate the template for it to work properly.
Right click on your Sandbox--> Sandbox settings
Navigate to Applications---> All Applications
Remove Roboform
Apply, Ok
In Sandboxie Control --> Configure --> Reload configuration
