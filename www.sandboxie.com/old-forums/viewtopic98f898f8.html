Hi phrab, the dots and the red X works the same now as years ago.

When you see red dots in the Sandboxie icon, it means programs are running in the sandbox. If you dont see them, it means there are no programs running in the sandbox.

When you see a Red X, it means the sandbox contents are being deleted. Deleting the sandbox can be done mannually or set to be done automatically.

In the example you give, it appears to me that you are using a sandbox thats not set to delete on closing of the sandboxed program. So, when you close the application, programs running in the sandbox are terminated (disappearing red dots) but the sandbox contents are not deleted but are being saved (no Red X).

Bo
