Barb,

Thank you for your response.

I could not find anything in the forum via the search link you created to specifically address the issue I was having.

I did however spend some time playing around with the Preferences of FoxitPhantom and eventually I found the cause of the Sandbox not auto deleting.

For anyone else who may come across this, navigate to: Preferences - General - Application Startup and uncheck Show Start page. Well that solved it for me at least (Foxit Phantom Version: 8.3.5.30351)
