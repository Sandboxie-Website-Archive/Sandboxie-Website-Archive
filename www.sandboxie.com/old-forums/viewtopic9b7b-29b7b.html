  • Quote

Post by Curt@invincea » Mon Feb 22, 2016 11:32 am

Combined 32/64 installer:
http://www.sandboxie.com/SandboxieInstall.exe

Separate:
http://www.sandboxie.com/SandboxieInstall32.exe
http://www.sandboxie.com/SandboxieInstall64.exe

There are no code changes in 5.10 RC. We are releasing 5.10 quickly due to many users' concern about the confusing SHA1 certificate warnings issued by IE 11 and Windows 10 when installing Sandboxie 5.08.

Changes in 5.09.2
1) Fixed Win 10 FR Build 14271 (SBIE1113 Cannot find Nt system service, reason MASTER TABLE)

Changes in 5.09.1
1) Fixed Win 10 FR Build 14267 (SBIE1113 Cannot find Nt system service, reason MASTER TABLE)
2) Sandboxie binaries are now dual-signed (SHA1 & SHA256) to eliminate IE 11 and Win 10 certificate warnings.
