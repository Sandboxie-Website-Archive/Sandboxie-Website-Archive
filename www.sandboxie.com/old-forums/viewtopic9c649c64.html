Post by tzuk » Thu Jun 13, 2013 1:55 pm

Reason code 5 in message SBIE2303 means access denied, this is because GameGuard is
blocking modifications to program code and prevents Sandboxie from applying its hook.

I don't want to "fix" that in Sandboxie, because that would mean trying to actively subvert GameGuard,
and I have no wish of getting into some kind of arms race with the maker of Rappelz or GameGuard.

I suggest you contact the makers of Rappelz and ask them to reconsider this change to GameGuard.
tzuk
