SBIE2303 Could not hook CryptProtectData (1)
SBIE2318 DLL initialization failed for 'crypt32.dll'

Launching a different browse (internet explorer) sandboxed works without issue. If I close the error message, Firefox seems to work correctly in the sandbox anyway as far as I've seen, but I'm a little concerned about what the error means.
