Re: Chrome suddenly not working in Sandbox

Posted: Mon Apr 03, 2017 1:41 pm
by Curt@invincea
That error means that something in the sandbox tried to open a process and got ACCESS DENIED. More often than not, that is because the process being opened crashed or exited before it could be opened successfully. Most likely some add-in/plug-in does not like being run in the sandbox. Running ProcMon may show which process crashed.
