Bitdefender & Sandboxie compatibility solved

  • Quote

Post by biu26 » Fri Mar 01, 2013 12:41 pm

Dear Nyte,

I had the same issue with Bitdefender Internet Security 2013 preventing Google Chrome from accessing the Internet when opened within Sandboxie (64-bit, 3.74 and/ 3.76)

The solution I found was to exclude chrome.exe from the continuous anti-virus scanning by Bitdefender:
- settings-> anti-virus-> exclusions -> excluded processes -> add chrome.exe

This is the feedback I received from Bitdefender Customer Support:

Dear Customer,

Thank you for your reply.

Please be advised that the Active Virus Control function of Bitdefender will inject itself in every opened application and monitor it's activity.

This is designed to record the activity of the applications and see if they take suspicious actions.

Please let us know what version of Sandboxie are you using.

You can find this out by opening its main window and clicking Help->About Sandboxie from the top.

I would recommend you making sure that your version is the latest one (3.76) by reinstalling it, if necessary.
It seems that certain patches have been applied to Sandboxie so it can work alongside with Bitdefender's injection.

Also, by adding the trusted Google Chrome to Excluded Processes does not decrease the security status of your computer.

If you need further assistance, do not hesitate to ask.

Best regards,
Florin Radu
Bitdefender Technical Support Engineer

It seems excluding chrome.exe is a workable solution.
