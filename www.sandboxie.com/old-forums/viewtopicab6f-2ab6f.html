If you click on "run browser sandboxed" SBIE will load IE instead of Edge. This is the Edge is the Windows default.

If you click a link in email, it will open your default browser (Waterfox if you have changed that)...It will not be sandboxed unless you have your browser set to be "forced" to open in a SB.

You can set it to "force" Waterfox to open under the supervision of SBIE by invoking the Forced program option in the Licensed (paid) version of SBIE. http://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?Prog ... gs#program

Otherwise, it will open, but not sandboxed, unless you cut and past the link from your email, and open your browser (manually) in SBIE and pasting it there.
