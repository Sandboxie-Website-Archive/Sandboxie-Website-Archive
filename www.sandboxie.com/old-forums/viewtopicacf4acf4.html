Post by tzuk » Wed Dec 05, 2012 5:33 am

Your log mentions the program \program files (x86)\camfrog\camfrog video chat\crashsender.exe.
Going just by this program name, I suggest that CamFrog might be the component that crashes.

Let's try to disable CamFrog in the sandbox to see if it makes a difference.
Please open Sandbox Settings > Resource Access > File Access > Blocked Access,
click the Edit/Add button, then paste this:
*\camfrog\*

Click OK all the way back and restart IE in the sandbox.
