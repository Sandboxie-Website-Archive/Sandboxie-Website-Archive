Re: Firefox crashes after downloading files

Post by JoeHood » Tue Jan 19, 2016 5:41 pm

bjm wrote:Help me grasp why OMTC disabled may help.
Well remember that OMTC is only a suspect at this point. Anyway, OMTC is a layered form of rendering and involves the UI as well, particular in animations. OMTC is relatively new, so an outdated driver may not have the required technology.

In my case and as far as I have read here in this thread, all of our downloads are completing successfully. But the browser (Chrome/Firefox) is freezing at the end of the download. At the end of the download, there are animation and UI changes with both browsers. This most likely all works fine outside of Sandboxie.

Sandboxie introduces an additional UI change with the immediate recovery box and animation with the flashing frame. So we are seeing the freeze when sandboxed, and because that transition is now layered something is not being interpreted correctly probably with the driver. There may be a form of cache also with OMTC to explain why the first try at a download is the most likely to show the freeze and subsequent downloads fare better.
