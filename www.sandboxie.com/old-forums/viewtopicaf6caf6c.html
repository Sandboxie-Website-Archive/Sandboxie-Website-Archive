Post by Oileanai » Sat Jan 28, 2017 5:19 am

Hi folks,

I had the same issue: Upgraded Firefox to 51.0.1 (32-bit) and the pages loaded blank when using Sandboxie.
As per previous workaround, changing the Firefox security.sandbox.content.level to 0 resolves the issue.

For other greenhorns, to use this workaround:
Type "about:config" into the Firefox addressbar
Accept the risk.
Search for security.sandbox.content.level
Double click and change to 0

Thanks for many years of trouble free browsing with Sandboxie!
