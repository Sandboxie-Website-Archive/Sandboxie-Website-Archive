Sandbox man, for downloads to go straight through to an specific folder bypassing sandboxing, you need to allow Direct file access to those folders in Sandbox settings.

Sandbox settings>Resource access>File access>Direct access, Click "Add program", to add your browser. And now, Click "Add" for navigating to the folder where you want downloads to go straight through after downloading is finish bypassing sandboxing.
