email attachment won't open

  • Quote

Post by docfleetwood » Wed Aug 08, 2007 7:52 pm

Hello,

I am using Thunderbird email client and OpenOffice.org.

I received an email with two document attachments. I can open one of the attachments - i.e. double click and OpenOffice opens the file. However, I can not open a second attachment. A new instance of soffice.exe and soffice.bin startup in the sandbox but then nothing opens.

This is also true if I open a document in one email and then try to open another attached document in another email. This does not happen when I run Thunderbird not-sandboxed.

When sandboxed, in order to open a second attachment, I must kill one of the running soffice.exe processes and then, voila, the attachment that would not open before springs to life.

This happens even if I close OpenOffice after viewing the first document and then try to open the second. It seems that the sandbox does not 'release' the soffice.exe/soffice.bin programs.

I hope any of this was clear!

Any ideas?

Post by Paul_K » Thu Aug 09, 2007 10:12 am

Sorry, but I cannot duplicate your problem.
I created an email in my [EmailBox] using Thunderbird, attached 3 OpenOffice documents to it (2 - .odt Writer files, 1 - .ods Calc file) and then saved it to my Drafts folder. I also saved a copy to my hard drive, so I could open it (via File > Open Saved Message) in an different sandbox, with Thunderbird.

When I opened that saved email in the sandbox in which it was created, I double-clicked on one of the Writer file attachments and had 1 instance of soffice.exe and 1 instance of soffice.bin in the sandbox.
I then opened the Calc file, and still had only 1 instance of each soffice.xxx file.
I then opened the other Writer file, and I still only had 1 instance of soffice.exe and soffice.bin.
All 3 documents were open, and I had only 1 instance each of soffice.xxx.

I then switched to my [DefaultBox] and opened the copy of the email that I had saved to my hard drive: same results.

It shouldn't make any difference, but I do have 2 LingerProcess statements in the Sandboxie configuration for both sandboxes, as you may also have:
LingerProcess=soffice.exe
LingerProcess=soffice.bin
Since these statements are there to allow Sandboxie to automatically terminate these 2 processes, in the sandbox, after all other processes have ended, I don't see where it would have anything to do with the behavior that you are reporting.

Sorry, I have no answers for you. Maybe someone else...

Win XP Pro Sp2, Sandboxie 3.00.13, OpenOffice 2.2.1, Thunderbird 2.0.0.6
XP Pro SP3
