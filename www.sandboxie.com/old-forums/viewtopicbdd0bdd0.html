Post by Craig@Invincea » Fri Oct 02, 2015 9:10 am

Hi Everyone,
Well, I've finally been able to do some minor update work on the Sandboxie.com website. Currently, I've updated:

~Known Conflicts page http://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?KnownConflicts
(This will stay updated as we get more and more info..)

~FAQ (Technical Requirements Section) http://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?Freq ... dQuestions

The goal is to totally revamp the site. We inherited a good, but now dated site (originally written in June 2004.) Curt and I have some ideas to give some updates to the front page to quickly showcase what SBIE is and what it does well, obviously, if you go there now, that's not so certain. It's a guy sitting on a rock, on a mountain, with a laptop. http://www.sandboxie.com

With that, we also want update the graphics on many of the web pages, but especially the front page. (We're not changing the classic SBIE wedge/pizza graphics or programs icons...no need to worry!)

That will take time, as those have to be vetted.

Personally, I'd just like a brand-spanking new and modern site. We shall see.

-----------------------------------
Update as of 12/2015:
Two things:
1.We're getting some "swag" to give out for forum members that help us, help you(bugs, discoveries, being helpful, etc.) Some of you guys already have met that in spades! So.... I need Logo ideas. Of course, no payment would be offered (other than the swag in return) and we (Sandboxie Holdings) would be authorized to use the logo idea without restrictions. So not TM or copyright issues. We're thinking about shirts, etc. But We'd certainly give you props if we ended up using the idea!

2. Sandboxie Website. Meh. This site uses ancient phpbb code. It's archaic. While I'm doing my best to stumble on pages that need updating or just simply make zero sense anymore, please please feel free to point out anything (statement, page, etc) you may find that I need to fix. It's a collection of cobbled pages since 2004.

Known conflicts page will be updated constantly and the most frequently. The apps listed there will be used to trigger a compatibility box when the GUID is detected during the installation of SBIE to refer the user back to that page.
