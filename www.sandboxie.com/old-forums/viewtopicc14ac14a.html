Hello All

On two machines, web hyperlinks in Word 2016 (Click to Run 365, not itself sandboxed) will not open in IE11 (forced-sandboxed on Win 10). When a link is clicked, iexplore.exe shows for about 15 seconds under the sandbox in SB Control window and then disappears - Word 2016 just hangs and has to be closed via TM.

I've tried creating new sandboxes in 5.16 and also 5.17.1 and problem persists.

Web hyperlinks open fine from Word 2016 into non-sandboxed IE.
Outlook 2016 hyperlinks open fine into sandboxed IE.
Mail hyperlinks also open fine from Word 2016 to non-sandboxed Thunderbird client.

I'm aware that the links can just be copied from the documents and pasted into IE, but any help with 'Open DOCX hyperlinks direct to sandboxed IE' would be appreciated...

Strangely, on a third machine, running Win 7, hyperlinks open fine from Word 2016 - straight into sandboxed IE (same sandbox settings) using both 5.16 and 5.17.1.
