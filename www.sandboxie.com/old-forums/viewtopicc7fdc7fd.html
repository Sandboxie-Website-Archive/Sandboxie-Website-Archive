With more searching, I found the answer to this problem: http://forums.sandboxie.com/phpBB3/view ... ve#p120679

[Template_Local_Chrome_QWAVEdrv_DirectAccess]
Tmpl.Title=Google Chrome
Tmpl.Class=Local
ProcessGroup=<ChromePrograms>,chrome.exe,dragon.exe,iron.exe,opera.exe,maxthon.exe,vivaldi.exe
OpenPipePath=<ChromePrograms>,\Device\QWAVEdrv

Template=Local_Chrome_QWAVEdrv_DirectAccess
