Hello vgishmasters,

Thanks for your feedback/suggestions.

Installing drivers is outside the scope of Sandboxie. You may want to consider using a VM for that purpose.
Here's a related thread that may be useful:
viewtopic.php?p=89230#p89230

Regarding your second question, Sandboxie cannot tell which programs are "unknown". It is not an Antivirus but an isolation environment.
If you are unsure about a program, you may want to consider forcing your downloads folder to be Sandboxed, that way you have a safety net if you forget to right-click --->run sandboxed when downloading/testing files/programs.

Regards,
Barb.-
