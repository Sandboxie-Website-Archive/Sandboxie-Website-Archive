I know you did, but according to that faq as well as a few post made by Ronen in the past on the same issue, you have to not just uninstall Sandboxie, but also uninstall at least one of your security apps too, the reason why is because according to your event viewer, Sandboxie is having a problem registering a process notification routine on your pc, and the reason why is because the system will only register a limited number of these routines, so you have to first uninstall Sandboxie, and then atleast one (maybe more) of your security apps, to "make room" so to speak for Sandboxie, after you removed Sandboxie and 1 or more of your security apps, you can then install Sandboxie and it should be able to work correctly, after Sandboxie is installed and working on your pc, then you can put the other security app(s) back on your pc and everything will be running fine and youll have all your security apps including Sandboxie running on your pc. Hope this makes sense to you
