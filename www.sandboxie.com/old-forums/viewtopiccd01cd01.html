Sandboxie failed to load due to Email Reader [SOLVED]

  • Quote

Post by yabbadoo » Wed Apr 06, 2016 6:37 am

After many years with no trouble, my Sandboxie suddenly stopped loading. The message was that it could not find Email Reader under launcher.ex. I repaired the program - no change. I uninstalled and reinstalled it - no change.

I eventually found :- http://forums.sandboxie.com/phpBB3/view ... hp?t=12873 and a post by Guest 10 dated May 01 2012 which gave a fix for another member that worked.

I applied the same fix :-
Sandbox Settings > Resource Access > File Access > Direct Access
"Add Program" button: launcher.exe
"Edit/Add" button: %DefaultSpoolDirectory%\*

It worked !. A million thanks to the brilliance of Guest 10.
Can anybody tell me why this problem suddenly happened in the first place and is it always going to happen with every update ?
