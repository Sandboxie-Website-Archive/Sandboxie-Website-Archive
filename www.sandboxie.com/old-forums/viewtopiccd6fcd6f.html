Post by Oliver » Fri May 04, 2007 10:20 am

Hi Saj,

go to "Help", "About Opera" to find the path for your profile folder. Ie. it's "C:\Documents and Settings\Username\Application Data\Opera", your settings would be:

Code: Select all

[OperaBox]

Enabled=yes
CopyLimitKb=32768

OpenFilePath=opera.exe,C:\Documents and Settings\Username\Application Data\Opera

AutoDelete=Yes
AutoRecover=Yes

ForceProcess=opera.exe

LingerProcess=acrord32.exe 
LingerProcess=jusched.exe 
LingerProcess=syncor.exe

RecoverFolder=%Desktop%

BlockDrivers=y
BlockFakeInput=y
If you don't want to activate the AutoRecover/RecoverFolder feature for your desktop, please delete "AutoRecover=Yes" and "RecoverFolder=%Desktop%". If you only want to unsandbox your bookarks please change the OpenFilePath= to

OpenFilePath=opera.exe,C:\Documents and Settings\Username\Application Data\Opera\opera6.adr

I have rebooted my pc and same thing happens.
I just tracked down the problem and found a fix.

For Opera 9.20 you have to add

Code: Select all

OpenFilePath=opera.exe,C:\Documents and Settings\Saj\Application Data\Opera\Opera9.2\profile\opera6.adr
OpenFilePath=opera.exe,C:\Documents and Settings\Saj\Application Data\Opera\Opera9.2\profile\*.tmp
to your Sandboxie.ini.

Sandboxed *.tmp in the profile directory result in this error message... strange, isn't it?
