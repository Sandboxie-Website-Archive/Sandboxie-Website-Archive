The built-in template gives waterfox.exe Direct Access to the folders and files at:
%AppData%\Mozilla\Firefox\Profiles\

If your profile isn't located underneath that path, and while you still have "Allow Direct Access to entire Waterfox profile folder" selected, go to Sandbox Settings > Applications > Folders.
With Waterfox in the "Select folder for" box, click the "Add" button and navigate to and select the profile folder for Waterfox.
Save your work.
