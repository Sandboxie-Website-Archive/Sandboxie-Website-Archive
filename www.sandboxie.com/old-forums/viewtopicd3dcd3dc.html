Hello tercinial,

Do you have any security software installed? (Such as an Antivirus).
If yes, disable your Antivirus and re-try deleting the contents of your Sandbox/ updating Sandboxie.

Otherwise, you can try to manually turn off the Sandboxie Service (Open Services as Admin --> Right click on Sandboxie Service --> Stop) and then use the Sandboxie installer to update Sandboxie.

For more info regarding the unable to delete contents, please have a look at the existing threads:
search.php?keywords=unable+delete+contents

Regards,
Barb.-
