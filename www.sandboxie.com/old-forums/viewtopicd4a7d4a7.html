smallpebble wrote:Hello all, I've been a registered Sandboxie user for about 6 years, and the other day decided to have a play with multiple sandboxes, instead of just using one.
Very often I have programs running in multiple sandboxes at the same time. But for extra security, when doing sensitive browsing, I terminate all programs in all sandboxes and open a fresh browsing session in a clean sandbox, after I finish doing banking, email or whatever, I immediately close the browser and delete the sandbox. Then go back to regular browsing. I dont mix sensitive and regular browsing. Doing that as well as not having programs running in multiple sandboxes when doing sensitive browsing are good practices.

One of the links posted by Barb, it says, "But it is still important to first stop all sandboxed activity in all sandboxes, for maximum protection."
