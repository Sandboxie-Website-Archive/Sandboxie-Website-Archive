Flinchman22,

We do not support Windows Vista, however, here are a few things to try:

Delete the contents of your Sandbox.
Try a new Sandbox.
Deactivate your AV protection (or add Sandboxie to the exclusion list).
Ensure you are using the latest stable versions of the browsers.
Deactivate addons.
Try the latest Sandboxie beta:
http://forums.sandboxie.com/phpBB3/view ... 59&t=23535

If you provide your browser, SBIE, and AV versions, we'll be able to better assist you.
Also, are you running a 64 or 32 bit OS?

Flinchman22,

I am not sure if this will do the trick, but you may want to give it a go (this information was shared by another user, I have not tested it myself):
Settings > Additional > Network > Encrypted Connections Scanning > Do Not Scan Encrypted Connections (this is for Kaspersky).

Also, here are the executables that you may need to add to your AV's exclusion list:
Start.exe""License.exe""SandboxieBITS.exe""SandboxieCrypto.exe""SandboxieDcomLaunch.exe""SandboxieRpcSs.exe""SandboxieWUAU.exe""SbieCtrl.exe""SbieIni.exe""SbieSvc.exe"

And here's more information regarding your AV and SBIE:
https://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?Kno ... #Antivirus

AND

http://forums.sandboxie.com/phpBB3/view ... 50#p124766

As for Vista, we do not officially support it. However, we will try to assist as much as possible.
