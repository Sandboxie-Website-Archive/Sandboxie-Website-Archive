Re: Disabling Sandboxie in Win Startup

by Craig@Invincea
Um, it's always been an option.

Sandboxie Control>Configure>untick "SBIE control when Windows starts"

And with any Windows program, you can edit any programs from autoloading. Depending on your OS, in Win 7 you can use msconfig.exe and then the startup tab to disable auto load programs.

If you want to stop services from starting, you can goto Services.msc and configure that as well.
