Remove WebKatana, it's not compatible. http://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?KnownConflicts

Sandboxie browser? you mean clicking on "run browser sandboxed?" OR right clicking your browser and running "as sandboxed?" With Dr. Web installed, neither option may function correctly.

Downloaded files within Sandboxie? Many ways. Quick or immediate recovery or use Files/Folders view in SBIE control http://www.sandboxie.com/index.php?FilesAndFoldersView They are downloaded into sandboxie folders there. and you're prompted for immediate recovery unless that has been turn off.
