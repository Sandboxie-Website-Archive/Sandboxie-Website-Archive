Post by Curt@invincea » Tue Jul 15, 2014 5:59 pm

The service is appropriately named ClickToRunSvc. You can't just disable it because this is what downloads the updates in the background. And it is much more complicated than just renaming the user. There are lots of user permissions issues. The comments about Hyper-V are referring to the App-V server -- not the client. Click-to-run has to be allowed to download data, or Office will not work.
