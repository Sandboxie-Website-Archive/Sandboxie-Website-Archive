UPDATE:
Please try these steps and see if this alleviates the issue:
Right click on your Sandbox ---> Sandbox settings
Resource Access ---> COM Access
Add {53BD6B4E-3780-4693-AFC3-7161C2F3EE9C}
Apply it, then go to Configure --> Reload Configuration
Clear the Cache in Opera.
Delete the contents of your Sandbox and try the sites again in a Sanboxed Opera.
