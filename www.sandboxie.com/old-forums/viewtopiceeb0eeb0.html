First point, v5 is a beta product. Errors are expected in a beta environment.

SandboxieRpcSs.exe is required for SBIE to work. It's a timing / race loading issue. Which directly relates to SandboxieRpcSs.exe and what it expects. The issue has always been present. It was magnified by Win10. Win10 introduced a lot of new ways on how things are assigned and processed in parallel per core. It's not just "and upgrade" from 8.1. It's far more than that.

This existed in 8.1. Just not as much. It was limited. However, 10 made it main stream. Tweaking the code in SBIE awoken it in 8.1. This was to be expected. The Devs are fully aware and stated this was something to be expected. Multiple releases have addressed it better and better. In our in house automated torture tests in happens very rarely (once in almost a 1000) and given the 10s of thousands of SBIE users, we're confident those stats are valid and representative.

AVG doesn't have anything directly to do with this error. But it's a program that loads, and could contribute to the racing problem

And we have let you know (the forum) . It's addressed in my posts regarding this (like my post to you) and in Curt's change log on what we are doing. Reason 4.2 works for you? It doesn't have to support Win 10 nor does it have all the coding to do that. This problem manifested from Win 10.
