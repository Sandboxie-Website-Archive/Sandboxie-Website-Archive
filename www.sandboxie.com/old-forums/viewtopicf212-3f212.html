There is a very small, simple program you can use for this purpose, and that is:
http://www.mitec.cz/wrr.html

Just use that program to open your reghive file, where you can then explore the registry keys, or you can even export the whole registry hive as a .reg file where you can then merge it with your real registry outside the sandbox.
