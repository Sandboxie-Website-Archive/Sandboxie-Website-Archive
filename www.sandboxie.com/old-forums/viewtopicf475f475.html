Loc364 wrote: ↑
Thu Aug 31, 2017 2:19 pm
Also, in the Sandboxie settings, the Adobe Acrobat Reader profile has a -- instead of a + and I can't change it. Is that normal?
Yes, its normal. FYI, when you see a setting with the -- , it means its a global setting. It was set via Configure>Software compatibility. To disable it, you also do it in Configure>Software compatibility. On the other hand, settings with a + next to them only apply to individual sandboxes. They are set and disabled in Sandbox settings.

Bo
