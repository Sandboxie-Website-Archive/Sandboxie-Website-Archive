This is the warning message when you try to use outlook express:
WARNING: Your mail program is about to start. Sandboxie is not configured to allow the program direct access to your mailbox files and preferences. Any new mail messages that are received to your computer will be stored in the sandbox and may not be easily recoverable.

It is recommended that you do not allow the mail program to start. Click ignore to ignore this warning and run your mail program anyway. Any other button cancels the operation.
So basically if you don't keep your mail on the server, it will all be deleted if the sandbox gets emptied. As for Opera 9 mail, I haven't tried it, do you have issues or are getting error messages?
