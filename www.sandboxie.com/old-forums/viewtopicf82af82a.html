There might be more than one way to go about it but I would use something like this.

ProcessGroup=<SBIExtra>,ProcessName1.exe,ProcessName2.exe
InjectDll=<SBIExtra>,*InsertPathtoDllHere*
InjectDll64=<SBIExtra>,*InsertPathtoDllHere*

I actually use something like this, but in reverse, to !exclude specific files (like the new origin) that don't work well with it.
