Hello cornflake,

Logged in users set their timezone during registration and can change it in their user control panel.
Once you select the desired time zone, you can pick the location that you want. For example, mine is set to UTC -05 - America/New York.

If you were using UTC -04, you should have received a suggestion next to your time zone to switch to -05.
