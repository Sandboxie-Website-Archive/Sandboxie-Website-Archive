Re: Multi-process Firefox 51 - Blank pages

  • Quote

Post by Barb@Invincea » Thu Feb 16, 2017 2:57 pm

Hello volescamper ,

Please launch Firefox outside Sandboxie and type about:config in the address bar
Click "I accept the risk"
At the top , you will see a search bar, use it to type the following (one at a time):
browser.tabs.remote.autostart
browser.tabs.remote.autostart.2

As they show up, one at a time, double click on them so they switch their Value to "false" .

Once both are set to false, relaunch Firefox outside the Sandbox and repeat the process to ensure they have not reverted back to "true".
Once you have confirmed they are both set to "false", close Firefox and launch it Sandboxed.

For more information about e10, please see this link:
https://wiki.mozilla.org/Electrolysis

Regards,
Barb.-

Post by bo.elam » Sun Feb 26, 2017 12:57 pm

You need to turnoff Multiprocess in Firefox to fix the issue. Read this thread.
http://forums.sandboxie.com/phpBB3/view ... 11&t=23902

To disable Multiprocess, open about:config and change preference "browser.tabs.remote.autostart" to false.
http://www.ghacks.net/2016/07/22/multi-process-firefox/
