Your post gives all the good reasons why nothing should be changed.

I speak from the experience of having my own business. As you might guess from my username, my name is Pete. I own a dog walking service here where I live. The name of the service is Davidsdogwalking. The name David was the previous owner. I often get asked why the name when my name is Pete. Some long time clients also ask me why I don't change it.

Three reasons. 1) out of respect to my friend David. 2) The name is established and has a reputation of quality service 3) Marketing. I have some current clients who had been clients when the business first started years ago. They moved away, and have years later returned and again needed our service. They only remembered one thing, the name. Had I changed it they never would have found it.

When Invincea took over Sandboxie, everyone was afraid that would spell the end of the program. To me the fact Invincea honored Tzuk and what he accomplished by not changing what he set up, showed me they were committed to carrying it forth, rather then killing.

That's why many long time users have the attitude towards new comers welcome to a great piece of software. If you don't like the logo, get over it.
